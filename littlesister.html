Cute Little Sister

The one on whom I can trust on secrets but not on food
Sometimes sweet sometimes rude
Sometimes innocent sometime a cheater
But always a creative innovator
A chatterbox always on
May it be dusk or dawn
Still a day without her will be boring
just like a day without tea in the morning
This may sound a little bitter
But no she's my cute little sister.

-Vidhyani Gandhi
