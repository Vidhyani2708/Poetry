Women

A women everyday gets judged
For her clothes and appearance
Yet gears up enough courage to face it all
And they say women are weak
A women fights everyday with the world for her rights
She fights everyday for her stand
She fights everyday for being a woman
And they say women are weak
A woman, every single moment fights for her safety
A woman every single night when traveling alone hopes that she is protected from the evils of the society
Yet she hides all this behind a a smile
And they say women are weak
A women bears the pain of 20 bones breaking all together while giving birth to a child
And they say women are weak
A women bleeds average 8 years of her life
And they women are weak
After learning about a woman's bravery
Do they still have the right to call a women weak?

-Vidhyani Gandhi
