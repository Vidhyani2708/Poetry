Let It Go

The thing that holds you back like the roots of the tree
Let it go and let it set you free
The pain that wrenches your heart
From you, let it get apart
The Grudges that run your mind
Don't let it make you confined
Because you belong to the sky
Where you fly high
So let it go
That's what I wanted you to know.

-Vidhyani Gandhi
