SHE

She is staring the stars
For hours and hours
Who knows in happiness or in sorrow from her scars
A tired hardworking soul with hope
Hoping for better days' scope
With no enlighenment of future
She sat there getting colder
But who is she?
Is it you or is it me?
Or is it everyone who is trying to tell their story?

-Vidhyani Gandhi
