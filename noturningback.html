No Turning Back

The best part of life's childhood
When the terms of the world aren't understood
The sparkle on one's face and the innocence of one's mind
When every act was kind
But all this is left behind
Where life ahead is undefined
There's no turning back
With many shocks and surprises to unpack
I wish I could return to the time
When everything was fine.

-Vidhyani Gandhi
