The Dusk Of The Day

Cold breeze rushes around
In the sky, beautiful shaped clouds surround
Birds flying and chirping
Its all so captivating
Different shades of the sky appears
By this a little tension of one disappears
The branches of the trees dance as the wind passes by
the dusk of the day is so endearing to one's eye.

-Vidhyani Gandhi
