Friendship

Distance is not but you are the concern
A secret box full of secrets
Still you can add one more
A toy that god made
Which would never fade
Listening to your stupid stuff
Yet wear a smile bluff
Endless fights, endless laugh and endless memories
On whom you can trust without any worries
The most stupid person of your life
But will always protect you from knife
Friendship isn't a big thing
But it is many small things.

-Vidhyani Gandhi
