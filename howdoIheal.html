HOW DO I HEAL?

I walk through the blaze
Can't find my way out of this maze
From the burns I feel
How do I heal?
Everything is blurred by my tears
Only one thing can help: Prayers
With this how do I deal?
From this how do I heal?
Even if the flames are extinguished?
Will my burns be diminished?
How do I conceal?
From this how do I heal?

-Vidhyani Gandhi
