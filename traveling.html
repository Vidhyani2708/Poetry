Traveling

A tranquil exposure to the world beyond imagination
Which calms our soul with its fascination
It prepares us for the next adventures of life
Sheilding us for a while for a while from al the stife
Sitting by the sore feels like the sea whispers the sound of waves
While looking at the hills it feels like they are teaching us to be brave
