I WRITE ABOUT...

I look upside
And write about downwards
I see rightwards
And Write about leftwards
I notice the happenings of the world
And write about the things blurred
I hear what others are saying
And write about the words fading
I write because I feel someone's listening
Or am I finally listening to myself?

-Vidhyani Gandhi
