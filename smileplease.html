SMILE PLEASE

For your caring mother
And for your responsible father
Could you smile please
For your cute sibling who's annoying
And your sweet friends who are irritating
Could you smile please
Then finally for yourself
And for everything you have with ease
Could you smile please?
Let us be grateful
For everything that's fateful
Cause when you are grateful
Life seems a less fateful.

-Vidhyani Gandhi
